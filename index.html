About Us

Sackie Preparatory school is envisioning and shaping the lives of students through vocational home learning. In the view of the COVID-19 pandemic, this institution is optimistic in providing home base educational support to students to ensure learning takes place during this extrordinary time. The aim is to ensure that students are occupied and engaged during this crisis as we need to stand in solidarity to protect our children.
